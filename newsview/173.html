Visit our trials

At our premises in Kano we are running continuous trials.

Purpose of these trials:

·      Screening new varieties on added value and adaptability for the Nigerian market throughout the year in both dry and rainy season

·      Demonstration of improved seeds, comparison of local varieties to new imported quality seeds, both open pollinated and hybrid varieties

·      Demonstration of growing techniques and equipment

·      Demonstration of drip and spray tube irrigation

·      Demonstration on use of water-soluble fertilizers

 

You are most welcome to visit these trials and discuss with our staff.

Visiting is only possible on appointment.

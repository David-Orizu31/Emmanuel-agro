Welcome to Emmanuel Agro Enterprise.

We are a Nigeria based supplier for the agricultural and horticultural industry.

Our main office is based in Lagos.

Our customers are farmers and agriculture businesses in all regions of Nigeria.

We are the sole importer and distributor of:

  • 100% water soluble fertilizers (The Netherlands)
  • Vegetable seeds from 'Enza Zaden', 'Majstro' and 'Pop Vriend Seeds' (The Netherlands)
  • Drip and spray tube irrigation equipment from San-Fu (Taiwan)
  • Seedling trays and mulch foil from SanFu (Taiwan)
  • Coco peat from Dutch Plantin (The Netherlands)

 

Next to this we assist in:

  • Soil- and irrigation water analysis
  • New and fairly used equipment and machinery (tractors, boom sprayers, planters, etc.)

 

Our aim: 

  • Become your preferred agribusiness partner and supplier
  • By caring for your business
  • Giving technical support to obtain better results

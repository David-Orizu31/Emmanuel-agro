AGRONOMIST

Vacancy

TERRATIGA LIMITED, a dynamic Agricultural and Agro-allied Trading company operating in West Africa with its regional office in Lagos Nigeria and Head office in The Netherlands, has a vacancy in Nigeria for the post of Agronomist.


Profile:

We are looking for an ambitious Female or Male who is a good team player, but also can function independently. The ideal candidate must be able to communicate in Hausa language. He or She should be responsible, accurate, proactive, analytical and is willing to relocate to any part of Nigeria.


Qualification:

To carry out your responsibilities, we give preference to candidates with at least Bsc. or HND, in Agricultural Science.


Experience:

Specific practical skills on crop production, Horticulture and farm management etc will be required. Experience in grain Production will be an advantage.


Remuneration:

Competitive salary according to qualification and experience.


Questions and Applications:

Kindly address your application to 4, Metal box road, Ogba industrial scheme, Lagos, Nigeria.
